How We Work

Built around your market. Run by us.

Every search starts with a map of where the right person is already working. Then we go find them.

The Process

From intake to offer

  1. 01

    Intake & Alignment

    We learn the business before we open the search. Org structure, culture, what the role actually requires to succeed.

  2. 02

    Build the Map

    Target companies, job titles, geographies, candidate profile. All defined before a single outreach. No guessing.

  3. 03

    Sourcing & Qualification

    We engage candidates personally. No blasts, no job ads. You see fewer candidates and better ones.

  4. 04

    Present, Offer & Close

    Profiles with context, not just resumes. We stay in through compensation, references, and counter-offers.

The Methodology

How we build the map

Phase two in detail. The difference between a targeted search and a resume blast.

  • The companies where top performers in your sector are already working and proven.

  • The exact titles and functional backgrounds that indicate success in the role.
  • Beyond the resume: leadership style, operational strengths, and fit with your organization.
  • People not actively searching but open to the right opportunity. Consistently the stronger pool.
