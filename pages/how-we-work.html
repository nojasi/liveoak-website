How We Work

Built around your market. Run by us.

Every search starts with a defined map -- the companies, titles, and geographies where the right person is already working. Then we go find them.

The Process

From intake to offer

  1. 01

    Intake & Alignment

    We learn the business before we open the search. Org structure, culture, what hasn't worked, what the role actually requires to succeed. The intake call is the foundation everything else is built on.

  2. 02

    Build the Map

    We define the target companies, job titles, geographies, and candidate profile before making a single outreach. No guessing. The map tells us exactly where to look.

  3. 03

    Sourcing & Qualification

    We engage candidates personally -- no blasts, no job ads. Everyone on our shortlist has been screened by us for background, motivation, and fit against the brief. You see fewer candidates and better ones.

  4. 04

    Present, Offer & Close

    We deliver profiles with context, not just resumes. We stay in through the offer -- compensation guidance, reference checks, counter-offer strategy. We don't disappear when the shortlist is done.

The Methodology

How we build the map

Phase two in detail. This is what separates a targeted search from a resume blast.

Target Competitor Mapping

We identify the companies where top performers in your sector are currently working -- direct competitors and adjacent firms where the right talent is already proven.

Geographic Parameters

We define the search area based on where qualified talent actually lives and what relocation looks realistic. Realistic pools, not inflated pipelines.

Target Job Titles

We define the exact titles and functional backgrounds that indicate success in the role, so outreach reaches people with directly relevant experience.

Ideal Candidate Persona

Beyond the resume, we define what success looks like -- leadership style, operational strengths, and fit with your organization's stage and culture.

Passive Candidate Focus

We target people who are not actively searching but open to the right opportunity. That pool is consistently higher quality than what's already on the market.

Adjacent & Stretch Profiles

When the primary pool is limited, we expand into adjacent industries and consider candidates who may not check every box but show clear potential to grow into the role.
